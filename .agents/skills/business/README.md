## Quick Reference

### Product Definition
- **Name:** TrustLayer - Transparent Financial Credit Intelligence
- **Core Value:** Makes financial credit scoring transparent, understandable, and improvable
- **Technology:** AI + Blockchain (Monad)
- **Users:** Anyone wanting to understand their or others' financial creditworthiness

### Key Innovations
1. AI-powered credit analysis of multi-wallet financial history
2. Blockchain-based transparency with on-chain scoring records
3. Explainable scoring factors with improvement recommendations
4. Usage-based payment model powered by Monad

### Why Monad
- Easiest and most flexible wallet ecosystem
- Perfect for high-frequency, low-cost transactions
- Pay-per-use model economically viable
- Full EVM compatibility for smart contracts
